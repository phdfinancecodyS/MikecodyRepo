## COPYWRITING NOTES FOR TONE/VOICE

**Do:**
- Use "I" and "you" (personal)
- Acknowledge the awkwardness/shame people feel ("Can't tell if you're fine or actually struggling?")
- Be honest about what this is and isn't (not a diagnosis, is a screening)
- Use short sentences when making serious points
- End sections with a tiny bit of hope or lightness

**Don't:**
- Use clinical jargon ("major depressive disorder," "anxiety spectrum")
- Be overly cheerful about serious topics
- Lead with statistics (save for TikTok videos)
- Make it feel like a medical form
- Ask for too much personal info upfront
