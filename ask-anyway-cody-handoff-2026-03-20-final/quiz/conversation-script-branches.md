# Conversation Script Branches for Quiz Funnel

Date: 2026-03-20
Purpose: Give users immediate language after low/moderate concern outcomes, then branch by response type.

## Offer Structure on Result Screen (Low + Moderate)

Show two teaser starters, then ask:
- What happened when you said this?

Response buttons:
1. They said yes
2. They denied it / got defensive
3. They changed the subject / avoided

---

## Teaser Starters (Shown to all low/moderate users)

Starter A:
- I care about you and I have noticed some changes. Can I ask you something direct?

Starter B:
- You matter to me. Are you thinking about suicide?

Follow-up prompt under starters:
- Pick what happened, and we will give you your next exact lines.

---

## Branch 1: They Said Yes (Affirmative)

Immediate response script:
- Thank you for telling me. I am really glad you said that out loud.
- I am here with you. You do not have to carry this alone.

Next-line script:
- Are you in immediate danger right now?
- Have you thought about how you would do it?
- Do you have access right now?

Stabilizing script:
- Thank you for being honest with me.
- I want us to get support now, together.
- Would you call 988 with me right now, or text HOME to 741741 while I stay with you?

If they agree:
- Good. I am staying with you while we do it.

If they hesitate:
- I hear you. We can do one step at a time. I am not leaving you alone in this.

Safety rule in UI:
- Show crisis actions first: Call 988, Text HOME to 741741, Call 911.
- No paid offer above crisis actions.

---

## Branch 2: They Denied It / Got Defensive

De-escalation script:
- I hear you. I am not trying to judge you or label you.
- I asked because I care about you and I want you safe.

Keep-door-open script:
- If today is not the day to talk, that is okay.
- I am still here, and you can tell me anytime.

Gentle re-check options:
- Can I ask one better question: have things felt unbearable lately?
- On a hard day, do you feel like you want to disappear?

Close-with-connection script:
- I care about you either way.
- Can I check in again tomorrow?

UI note:
- Offer one-button follow-up: Save this script.
- Offer optional next step: Send me 3 check-in texts I can use this week.

---

## Branch 3: They Changed the Subject / Avoided

Return-to-topic script:
- Before we move on, I want to come back to something important.
- I asked because you matter to me.

Direct but calm script:
- I want to be clear: are you having thoughts about suicide?

If they keep avoiding:
- You do not have to explain everything right now.
- I just need to know if you are safe tonight.

Bridge-to-support script:
- If talking to me feels hard, we can contact someone neutral together right now.
- We can call 988 and just start with: I am worried and not sure what to say.

UI note:
- Provide two buttons: Try one more direct line, Get support options now.

---

## Optional Micro-Flow After Each Branch

Prompt:
- Want the full version of this script set?

Options:
- Email me the full conversation map
- Text me a 24-hour check-in plan
- Continue without sharing

Principles:
- Never gate safety resources.
- Script delivery is convenience, not access.

---

## Compliance + Tone Guardrails

- Use plain language, not diagnosis language.
- Validate without promising outcomes.
- Do not include method details.
- Keep crisis support visible on every script screen.
- Keep sentence length short for mobile readability.
