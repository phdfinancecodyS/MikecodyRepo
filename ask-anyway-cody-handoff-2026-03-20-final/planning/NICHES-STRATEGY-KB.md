## Core Model from PDF

Hub and Spoke structure:
- 1 hub brand account/site
- up to 500 niche spokes
- each spoke uses same product architecture and delivery engine

Product stack per spoke:
- Guide: $2 one-time (PDF)
- Kit: $5 one-time (resource/crisis tools)
- Check On Me SMS: $5/month recurring
- Bundle: $10 one-time + $5/month option

Operating principle:
- reuse backend and delivery logic
- vary topic, audience language, and examples by spoke
- keep pricing simple and consistent

## Build Rules for Every Spoke

- keep quiz free
- show results immediately
- capture contact after results (optional)
- show crisis support before monetization for high-risk users
- use same product architecture and analytics events
- keep copy non-diagnostic and plain-language

## Metrics to Track Per Spoke

Acquisition:
- video views
- quiz starts
- quiz completion rate

Conversion:
- post-result email opt-in rate
- SMS opt-in rate
- Guide/Kit conversion rate
- bundle attach rate

Safety and quality:
- crisis CTA click-through rate
- drop-off rate on high-risk result pages
- user feedback sentiment

## Decision Framework for New Spokes

A niche qualifies if:
- it has active creator communities (TikTok/IG/Reddit)
- there is clear pain and help-seeking behavior
- message can stay safety-compliant and non-sensational
- same product templates can be adapted without rewriting backend
