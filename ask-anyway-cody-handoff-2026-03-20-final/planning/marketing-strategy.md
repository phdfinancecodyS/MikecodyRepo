# TikTok + Affiliate Marketing Strategy

## Overview

TikTok is built for this topic. Mental health content consistently goes viral because it's personal, relatable, and shareable. The strategy is:

1. **Organic content builds trust → Affiliates amplify reach → Traffic converts on sales page**

Your clinical credibility (psych NP) is the differentiator. You're not a wellness influencer — you're a clinician making real tools accessible.

**Program positioning:** Everyone says "start the conversation." Nobody teaches you how. That's the gap — and that's the hook for every piece of content.

### TikTok Posting Cadence (Recommended)

- **Minimum viable:** 3x/week
- **Growth phase:** 5–7x/week
- **Format mix:** 60% educational talking-head or text-overlay, 30% storytelling/narrative, 10% trending audio + mental health message

### Content-to-Sale Ratio

Do NOT pitch in every video. Target ratio: **10:1** (10 free value videos for every 1 direct sales mention). The affiliate links do the selling for you passively.

## TikTok-Specific Tactics

### Algorithm Optimization
- **Hook in first 2 seconds** — state the problem or promise immediately
- **Caption:** Use question format to drive comments ("Have you ever noticed this in someone you love?")
- **Hashtags:** Mix large (#mentalhealth, #suicideprevention, #mentalhealthawareness) with niche (#QPR, #safetyplanning, #psychNP, #crisisintervention)
- **Pinned videos:** Pin your best performing educational piece, your "who I am" intro, and the lead magnet CTA

### TikTok Shop / Stan Store
- List the program directly in TikTok Shop if available in your region (no link-in-bio friction)
- Stan Store is the #1 recommended platform for creators — integrates directly with TikTok bio link

### Comments Strategy
- Respond to every comment in the first hour (boosts distribution)
- Use "reply to comment" video format for FAQs — these perform extremely well in mental health niche

## Safe Messaging on TikTok

TikTok has specific policies around suicide/self-harm content. To stay compliant AND safe:

- Do NOT use specific methods, statistics framed sensationally, or graphic imagery
- DO use safe messaging language: "died by suicide" not "committed suicide"
- Always include crisis resources in videos where crisis topics are discussed: **988** and **Crisis Text Line (text HOME to 741741)**
- Review [AFSP Safe Messaging Guidelines](https://afsp.org/safe-messaging-guidelines) before publishing
