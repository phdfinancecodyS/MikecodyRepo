# Affiliate Program Structure

## Overview

An affiliate program lets other TikTok creators, mental health advocates, therapists, and coaches earn a commission for every sale they drive. This is how you scale reach without ad spend.

---

## Commission Structure (Recommended)

| Tier | Who | Commission | Notes |
|------|-----|------------|-------|
| **Standard Affiliate** | Anyone who applies | 30% | Industry standard for digital products |
| **Mental Health Pro Affiliate** | Therapists, counselors, coaches, NPs | 40% | Higher trust, higher conversion |
| **VIP / Launch Affiliate** | First 20 affiliates | 50% for 90 days → 40% ongoing | Incentivize early adoption |

**Example:** $47 program × 30% = **$14.10 per sale**. A TikToker with 10K followers driving 10 sales/month earns $141 passively.

---

## How Affiliates Promote

- TikTok video with affiliate link in bio (most common)
- TikTok stitch or duet of your content with their commentary
- Instagram Reels + link in bio
- YouTube Shorts
- Email list (therapists/coaches with newsletters)
- Pinterest (underrated for mental health content — evergreen)

## Affiliate Onboarding Kit Contents

The `/marketing/affiliate/` folder will contain:

1. **Welcome Letter** — What the program is, why it matters, how to earn
2. **Swipe Copy** — Pre-written captions for TikTok, Instagram, email
3. **Approved Talking Points** — What affiliates CAN and CANNOT say (crucial for mental health content)
4. **Safe Messaging One-Pager** — Non-negotiable guidelines for all affiliates
5. **Graphics Pack** — Canva templates for promo posts
6. **FAQ for Affiliates** — Payout schedule, cookie duration, support contact

---

## Safe Messaging Requirements for Affiliates

**All affiliates MUST agree to:**
- Use "died by suicide" language only (not "committed suicide")
- Never describe or imply methods
- Always include crisis resources in any post that references crisis (988)
- Not make medical claims about the program
- Not fabricate testimonials or results
- Follow AFSP safe messaging guidelines

**Affiliates who violate these terms are immediately removed.**

## Recruitment Strategy

### Phase 1: Seed Affiliates (Before Launch)
- Identify 10–20 TikTok creators in mental health, therapy, wellness space (5K–100K followers range performs best — higher engagement than mega-influencers)
- Offer VIP 50% commission for first 90 days
- Give free access to the program to review/promote authentically
- Focus on therapists and counselors who already have trust audiences

### Phase 2: Open Affiliate Program
- Add affiliate sign-up link to sales page
- Mention affiliate program in TikTok videos ("DM me 'AFFILIATE' if you want to earn promoting this")
- List on affiliate directories: PartnerStack, ShareASale (after scaling)

---

## Payout Schedule

- PayPal or direct deposit
- 30-day cookie window (standard)
- Minimum payout: $20
- Payout schedule: 1st of each month, 30-day delay (to account for refunds)

## Tracking and Analytics

Track affiliate performance in the sales platform dashboard:
- Clicks per affiliate
- Conversion rate per affiliate
- Revenue per affiliate
- Top-performing content types (identify what's working for top affiliates and share as best practices)

## Affiliate Agreement — Key Clauses

*(To be drafted into a proper legal document before launch)*

1. Affiliates represent they will comply with AFSP safe messaging guidelines
2. No false or misleading claims about health outcomes
3. Disclosure requirement: all promotional content must include #ad or #sponsored or "affiliate link" disclosure (FTC compliance)
4. Commission forfeiture on refunded sales
5. Termination clause for safe messaging violations
