## OVERVIEW

Instead of one generic pitch, we'll have multiple "Why partner with us?" narratives tailored to creator demographics and audiences. This increases conversion when reaching out to creators because it speaks to THEIR values and challenges, not just ours.

**Example:** A male first responder with 15k followers sees a pitch about helping his community through mental health education. A female nurse with 20k followers sees a pitch about supporting healthcare workers under stress. Different pitch, same program.

## PITCH STRUCTURE (Each Variant)

### Section 1: HOOK
**What it speaks to:** Their specific pain point or value
- "You see it every day in your line of work..."
- "Your followers trust you with real conversations..."
- "You want to make a difference in your community..."

### Section 2: THE PROBLEM (in their context)
**What it highlights:** Data/reality specific to their demographic
- First responders: 18% higher suicide rate than general population
- Nurses: 4x higher burnout than other professions
- Teachers: Students disclose to them before school counselors
- Parents: 70% wish they had better tools to talk to kids

### Section 3: THE SOLUTION (what we offer)
**How it frames:** Solution + their role
- "Here's a program designed specifically for [their audience]. You can share it authentically because you live this."
- Testimonials or data from *their* demographic using the program

### Section 4: WHY YOU
**What it emphasizes:** Our credibility *for their demographic*
- LCSW credential
- Co-author's real loss experience
- Affiliate commission structure ($20-$75 per video)
- They keep their full audience relationship (not white-labeled)

### Section 5: THE ASK
**What it wants:** Clear, low-friction call-to-action
- "Record one video. Here's what works. We handle the conversions."
- Payment terms (when they get paid)
- Exclusivity (or not) — can they promote competitors?

### Section 6: NEXT STEPS
**What it offers:** Immediate support
- "Let's do a call this week to discuss your audience"
- Sample video + script provided
- Payment processed within 5 business days of posting

## NOTES FOR LATER

- **Authenticity first:** These pitches should feel like opportunities, not sales. Creators can smell inauthenticity.
- **Don't oversell:** Let the program speak. "Your audience trusts you. Here's something to share."
- **Commission is secondary:** Lead with impact (helping their community), commission is the thank-you.
- **One pager best:** Keep the outreach email to ~3 paragraphs. Save the deck for calls/follow-up.
- **Personalization wins:** If you can mention their specific audience or recent video, do it. "I saw your video about X — this is perfect for that community."
