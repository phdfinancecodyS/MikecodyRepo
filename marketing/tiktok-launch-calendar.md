## TIKTOK CTA LANGUAGE

Use CTA language that feels direct and useful, not clever or manipulative.

Best options:
- "If you want a quick check-in, the quiz is in my bio."
- "If you want to see where you're at, take the free quiz in my bio."
- "I made a free quiz that gives you your result right away. Link in bio."
- "If this sounds like you, take the quiz in my bio and see what support fits."
- "If you're worried about yourself or someone else, the free quiz is in my bio."

Avoid:
- "Unlock your results"
- "Find out the truth about yourself"
- "This quiz will change your life"
- "Comment QUIZ and I'll send it"
- "Don't miss this"

Rule of thumb:
- say what it is
- say it is free
- say they see the result right away
- send them to the bio link
