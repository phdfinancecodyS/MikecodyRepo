## When to Use These

Send after a creator replies "yes" or "tell me more" to your cold DM. This is the pitch email  - it covers everything they need to decide. Do not send the one-pager without this email first; this email frames the one-pager so they read it correctly.

**Subject lines (test and use one):**

- Here's how the Ask Anyway affiliate program works
- Details from [your name]  - Ask Anyway
