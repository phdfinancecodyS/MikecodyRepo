# Ask Anyway — Affiliate Compensation Structure

Date: 2026-03-20
Status: Active

---

## Why Pure Commission (Not Flat Fee) at Launch

At launch you have no verified conversion data. Paying a flat fee per post before you know what a post actually converts is throwing money at unknowns. Pure commission aligns incentives — creators only earn when you earn — and it protects you while the funnel is still being validated.

Once you have 30 days of conversion data showing average earnings per post by niche, you can introduce flat-fee deals for high-performing creators as a retention tool.

---

## Commission Tiers

| Tier | Who Qualifies | Rate | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Standard** | Any approved affiliate | 30% | Default rate for all new creators |
| **Launch Affiliate** | First 30 affiliates to sign up | 40% for first 90 days, then 30% | Incentivizes early adoption |
| **Mental Health Pro** | Licensed therapists, counselors, social workers, NPs, psychologists | 40% ongoing | Trust signal — their audience converts higher |
| **Top Performer** | Any affiliate with 50+ sales in a calendar month | 50% that month | Resets monthly, keeps top earners motivated |

## What 30% Earns Per Product

| Product | Price | Affiliate Earns (30%) | Affiliate Earns (40%) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Single guide | $9 | $2.70 | $3.60 |
| Kit | $19 | $5.70 | $7.60 |
| Bundle (3 guides + kit) | $34 | $10.20 | $13.60 |
| SMS (monthly) | $4.99/mo | $1.50/mo | $2.00/mo recurring |

SMS commission pays every month the subscriber stays active — this is the sleeper value for creators who build an engaged audience.

---

## Realistic Earnings Example for a Nano Creator

One post. 5,000 followers. First responder niche. 3 sales of the bundle ($34).

- At 30%: $30.60 from one post
- At 40% (launch rate): $40.80 from one post

That's not life-changing alone. The pitch is volume over time, not one post. A creator who posts once a month for a year at 3 bundle sales per post earns $370–$490 passively for 12 videos.

## Free Product Policy

Every affiliate gets free access to the quiz and one complete guide kit before posting. Non-negotiable. They cannot authentically promote something they haven't used.

- Nano and micro creators: one kit of their choice, free
- Mental health pros: full platform access, free
- This is not optional and is not charged back regardless of performance

---

## Payment Terms

- Minimum payout: $25 (prevents micro-payment processing overhead)
- Payment schedule: Monthly, by the 15th of the following month
- Payment method: Stripe, PayPal, or direct deposit (Cody to configure)
- Cookie duration: 30 days (someone who clicks their link has 30 days to purchase and the affiliate gets credit)
- Attribution: last-click wins

---

## What Affiliates Cannot Do

All affiliates must agree to these before they receive their link. Anyone who violates is removed immediately.

**Safe messaging (non-negotiable):**
- Never say "committed suicide" — use "died by suicide"
- Never describe or reference methods
- Always include 988 or a crisis resource in any post that references suicidal crisis
- Do not imply the product prevents suicide or guarantees outcomes
- Follow AFSP safe messaging guidelines (link will be in onboarding kit)

**Content rules:**
- Do not fabricate testimonials or results
- Do not make medical or treatment claims about the product
- Do not use fear or crisis imagery to drive clicks
- All promotional content must disclose the affiliate relationship (#ad or "partner" in caption)

**Platform rules:**
- FTC disclosure required on all monetized posts
- No spam, cold messaging to their audience, or aggressive CTAs

## When to Introduce Flat Fees

Add flat-fee options once you have this data:

- 30+ active affiliates with at least one post each
- Average earnings per post tracked by niche
- At least one niche showing 10+ sales per post per creator

At that point you can offer: **flat fee + lower commission** to top-performing mid-tier creators who want predictability. Suggested structure when ready: $75–$150 flat per dedicated post + 15% commission.

Do not offer flat fees before this threshold. There is no data to justify them yet.
