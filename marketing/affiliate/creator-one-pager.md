## What Ask Anyway Is

Ask Anyway is a short, practical guide program for people who are ready to ask for help — but keep not doing it. It starts with a free quiz that figures out where someone is, what's holding them back, and what kind of support would actually fit their life. From there, they get a guide written for people like them.

It was built by a licensed clinical social worker and psychiatric nurse practitioner. It is not therapy. It is the step before therapy — written for the person who has been on the edge of asking for months.

## Who It's For

The person who:
- Knows they need support but doesn't know who to ask or how
- Has Googled "therapist near me" and then closed the tab
- Feels like they can't take the first step for reasons they can't fully explain
- Lives in a culture where asking for help is seen as weak (first responders, military, healthcare, high-performance professions)

## What Not to Say — Hard Rules

**Safe messaging (required for all posts):**
- Do not use "committed suicide" — say "died by suicide" if you reference it at all
- Do not describe, reference, or imply suicide methods in any post promoting this program
- If you bring up suicidal crisis, always include 988 or the Crisis Text Line (text HOME to 741741) in your caption or video
- Do not imply Ask Anyway prevents suicide, treats mental illness, or replaces clinical care
- Follow AFSP safe messaging guidelines: [https://afsp.org/safe-messaging-guidelines](https://afsp.org/safe-messaging-guidelines)

**FTC disclosure (required by law):**
- Every monetized post must disclose the affiliate relationship
- Accepted formats: `#ad`, `#partner`, `#sponsored`, "paid partnership with Ask Anyway", or equivalent disclosure in the first line of the caption
- Buried disclosure or disclosure in comments does not satisfy this requirement

**Other hard stops:**
- Do not fabricate results, testimonials, or statistics
- Do not make medical or treatment claims ("this will cure," "this treats," "this diagnoses")
- Do not use scare tactics, crisis imagery, or graphic mental health content to drive clicks

Violating any of these results in immediate removal from the program and forfeiture of unpaid commissions. This is not punitive — it's the standard applied to all affiliates equally.
