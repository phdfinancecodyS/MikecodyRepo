# Betrayed by the System: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** First Responder

**From guide:** Betrayed by the System

---

Goal: Name what the system did and track what it's costing you now.

Prompts:
- What did the system promise you? What did it actually deliver?
- When you think about what happened, what's the dominant feeling: rage, disgust, grief, betrayal, numbness?
- Where does the anger go when you can't direct it at the institution? (Partner, kids, self, substances?)
- What institution do you still have to interact with that triggers this? How do you get through it?
- What would accountability look like: and do you believe it's possible?
- What parts of the job follow you home most often: irritability, numbness, sleep disruption, scanning, or disconnection?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
