# How To Ask for Help Without Feeling Weak: Ask Script Builder

**Audience:** LGBTQ+

**From guide:** How To Ask for Help Without Feeling Weak

---

Goal: Write your exact ask so you can deliver it without overthinking.

Prompts:
- The person I'm going to ask is: ___. I chose them because: ___.
- My ask will sound like: "I need help with ___. Can you ___ by ___? If you can't, no pressure: I've got a backup plan."
- I'll ask them by: ___ (in person, text, call). The best time is: ___.
- If they say no, I'll say: "No problem. Thanks for being honest." Then I'll ask: ___ as my backup.
- After they help, I'll follow up with: ___ (thank you text, returned favor, acknowledgment). Closing the loop matters.

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
