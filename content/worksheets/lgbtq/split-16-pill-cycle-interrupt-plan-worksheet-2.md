# Pill-Cycle Interrupt Plan: Action Builder

**Audience:** LGBTQ+

**From guide:** Pill-Cycle Interrupt Plan

---

Goal: Take one honest step toward breaking the pill cycle.

Prompts:
- Today I'll take my medication exactly as prescribed. The hardest moment will be ______.
- When the urge to take more hits, I'll do ______ for 10 minutes first.
- One person I'll tell about this pattern (doctor, sponsor, partner, therapist):
- The question I'll ask my doctor: "Is there a safer way to manage ______?"
- By this weekend, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
