# Hopelessness First Aid: Action Builder

**Audience:** LGBTQ+

**From guide:** Hopelessness First Aid

---

Goal: Turn what you noticed into one small action and one lifeline.

Prompts:
- One thing I'll do in the next hour that proves I can still act:
- The person I'll text today, even if all I say is "hard day":
- When the "nothing matters" thought starts, I'll say to myself:
- One thing I used to care about that I'm willing to try again, even for five minutes:
- By tomorrow night, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
