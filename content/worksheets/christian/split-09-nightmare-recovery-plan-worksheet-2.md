# Nightmare Recovery Plan: Action Builder

**Audience:** Christian

**From guide:** Nightmare Recovery Plan

---

Goal: Build a post-nightmare recovery plan and one sleep-safety strategy.

Prompts:
- My wake-up protocol: feet on floor, lights on, then ______ (cold water, name 5 things, call someone).
- One safe image I'll practice visualizing before sleep tonight:
- One thing I'll change about my sleep environment to make it feel safer:
- One person I'll tell about the nightmares this week:
- By this weekend, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
