# Moral Injury 101: Action Builder

**Audience:** Christian

**From guide:** Moral Injury 101

---

Goal: One step toward moral repair: not erasure, not forgiveness, just repair.

Prompts:
- One thing I'll do this week that aligns with who I want to be now (not who I was then):
- The sentence I'll say when the guilt loop starts: "What happened was ______. What I'm doing now is ______."
- One person I trust enough to tell one piece of this story to:
- What repair looks like for me (amends, service, honesty, therapy, writing):
- By next week, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
