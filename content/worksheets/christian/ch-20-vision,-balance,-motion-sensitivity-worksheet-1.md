# Vision, Balance, Motion Sensitivity: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Christian

**From guide:** Vision, Balance, Motion Sensitivity

---

Goal: Track your vestibular triggers: what makes it worse, what helps.

Prompts:
- When does the dizziness, nausea, or imbalance spike: screens, driving, head turns, crowds?
- How long do episodes last? Minutes, hours, or ongoing?
- What makes it better: sitting still, dark room, fixed-point focus, or something else?
- Have you had this evaluated by a doctor? What did they find or not find?
- How is this affecting your work, driving, or daily routine?
- How does faith language help this problem, and where does it make you hide instead of tell the truth?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
