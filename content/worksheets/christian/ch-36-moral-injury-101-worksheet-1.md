# Moral Injury 101: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Christian

**From guide:** Moral Injury 101

---

Goal: Name the violation: what happened, what code it broke, and what it costs you now.

Prompts:
- What did you do, witness, or fail to prevent that violates your moral code?
- What does the shame say about you? Write the exact sentence your brain repeats.
- Who can't you face because of what happened? What do you imagine they'd say if they knew?
- What have you tried to do with the guilt: bury it, punish yourself, justify it, confess it?
- Is the person who did that thing the same person sitting here now? What's changed?
- How does faith language help this problem, and where does it make you hide instead of tell the truth?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
