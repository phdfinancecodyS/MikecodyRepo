# 3am Wake Loop Reset: Action Builder

**Audience:** Grief / Loss

**From guide:** 3am Wake Loop Reset

---

Goal: Build a 3am protocol that breaks the loop without fighting it.

Prompts:
- My 3am rule: no phone, no problem-solving. Instead I'll ______.
- My brain dump tool (notepad, voice memo): I'll put it ______ before bed tonight.
- One daytime change I'll make (caffeine cutoff, worry time, exercise timing):
- One thing I'll tell my doctor or therapist about this pattern:
- By this weekend, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
