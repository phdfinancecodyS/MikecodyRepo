# Work Identity Loss Plan: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Grief / Loss

**From guide:** Work Identity Loss Plan

---

Goal: Map the loss: what the job gave you and what's missing now.

Prompts:
- What did the job give you besides money: purpose, identity, structure, status, belonging?
- When someone asks "what do you do?" what happens inside you?
- What have you been doing to fill the time? Is it working or just filling?
- What skill or value of yours has nothing to do with a job title?
- What scares you most about what comes next: failure, irrelevance, starting over, or being seen?
- How is grief changing your energy, concentration, relationships, or stress tolerance right now?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
