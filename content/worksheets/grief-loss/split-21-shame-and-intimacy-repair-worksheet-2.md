# Shame and Intimacy Repair: Action Builder

**Audience:** Grief / Loss

**From guide:** Shame and Intimacy Repair

---

Goal: Turn what you noticed into one brave step toward being seen.

Prompts:
- One honest thing I can tell my partner about what happens when I get close:
- The sentence I'll say when I notice shame shutting me down: "I'm here, and ______"
- One small way I'll practice being seen this week without performing:
- What I need from my partner when shame shows up (space, patience, eye contact, words):
- By tomorrow night, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
