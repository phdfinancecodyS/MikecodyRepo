# Emotional Numbness: Action Builder

**Audience:** Grief / Loss

**From guide:** Emotional Numbness

---

Goal: Create one small opening for feeling without forcing it.

Prompts:
- One song, memory, or image that used to move me that I'll try today:
- One body movement I'll do for five minutes (walk, stretch, cold water, dance):
- The person I'll tell "I've been numb lately": even by text:
- What I'll do when numbness feels unbearable instead of numbing harder:
- By tomorrow night, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
