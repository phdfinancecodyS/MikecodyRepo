# Money Avoidance Reset: Action Builder

**Audience:** Grief / Loss

**From guide:** Money Avoidance Reset

---

Goal: Open one financial door and survive it.

Prompts:
- The one money thing I'll look at today (bank balance, one bill, credit card statement): ______.
- The number I'm most afraid of: ______. The actual number: ______.
- One call, email, or payment I'll make this week to stop the bleeding:
- One person I'd be willing to say "I'm behind on money stuff" to:
- By this weekend, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
