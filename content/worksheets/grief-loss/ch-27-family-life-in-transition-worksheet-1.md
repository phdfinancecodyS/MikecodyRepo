# Family Life in Transition: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Grief / Loss

**From guide:** Family Life in Transition

---

Goal: Name the transition clearly and see who's struggling with what.

Prompts:
- What changed: separation, retirement, move, new household structure, role shift?
- Who in the family is adjusting fastest? Who's having the hardest time?
- What old routine disappeared that the family hasn't replaced yet?
- What's the biggest source of friction in the new arrangement?
- What does each family member need most right now that they aren't getting?
- How is grief changing your energy, concentration, relationships, or stress tolerance right now?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
