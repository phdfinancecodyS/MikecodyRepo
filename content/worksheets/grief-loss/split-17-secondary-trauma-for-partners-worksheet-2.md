# Secondary Trauma for Partners: Action Builder

**Audience:** Grief / Loss

**From guide:** Secondary Trauma for Partners

---

Goal: Reclaim one piece of your own life this week.

Prompts:
- One thing I'll do this week that's exclusively for me: ______.
- One boundary I need to set with my partner's struggle: "I can't ______ anymore, but I can ______." 
- One person I'll talk to about MY stuff (friend, therapist, support group): ______.
- One thing I'll stop apologizing for needing (sleep, space, fun, help):
- By this weekend, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
