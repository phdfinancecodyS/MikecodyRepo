# Screen and Scroll Loop Reset: Action Builder

**Audience:** Single Parent

**From guide:** Screen and Scroll Loop Reset

---

Goal: Break one scroll trigger with one replacement action.

Prompts:
- My highest-risk scroll time is ______. My replacement for that window: ______.
- One app I'll remove from my home screen or set a timer on:
- My phone-free zone or time: ______ (bedroom, meals, first hour of morning).
- What I'll do with my hands instead (book, walk, stretch, cook, call someone):
- By this weekend, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
