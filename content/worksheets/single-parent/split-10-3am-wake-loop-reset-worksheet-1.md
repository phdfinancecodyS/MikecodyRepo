# 3am Wake Loop Reset: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Single Parent

**From guide:** 3am Wake Loop Reset

---

Goal: Map the 3am pattern: what wakes you and what your brain does next.

Prompts:
- What time do you wake up? Is it the same time every night?
- What's the first thought when your eyes open? Write it exactly.
- Does the waking feel physical (pain, heart racing, temperature) or mental (thoughts, dread, planning)?
- What do you reach for: phone, food, substances, nothing? What happens after?
- How many nights per week does this happen? What's it costing you?
- When does this problem hit hardest in the rhythm of parenting, and what is happening around it?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
