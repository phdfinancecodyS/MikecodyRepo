# Red/Yellow/Green Communication: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Faith Beyond Christian

**From guide:** Red/Yellow/Green Communication

---

Goal: Learn to read emotional zones: yours and theirs: before starting hard conversations.

Prompts:
- What color are you usually in when fights start: red, yellow, or green?
- What does your partner's red look like? What about their yellow?
- What triggers push you from green to yellow fastest?
- When's the last time you had a conversation where both of you were green? What was different?
- What do you usually do when someone is in red: push harder, withdraw, or match their energy?
- What parts of your faith or spiritual life make you feel steadier, and what parts make you feel pressure right now?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
