# Headache Functional Plan: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Faith Beyond Christian

**From guide:** Headache Functional Plan

---

Goal: Track the headache pattern: frequency, triggers, and what you're doing (or not doing) about it.

Prompts:
- How often are the headaches hitting? Daily, weekly, random?
- What's the pattern: tension (band around head), migraine (one side, light sensitivity), or pressure?
- What triggers them: stress, screen time, sleep, dehydration, jaw clenching, skipped meals?
- When a headache hits, what do you do? Power through, hide, medicate, nothing?
- What would you need to change to cut the frequency in half? Be honest.
- What parts of your faith or spiritual life make you feel steadier, and what parts make you feel pressure right now?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
