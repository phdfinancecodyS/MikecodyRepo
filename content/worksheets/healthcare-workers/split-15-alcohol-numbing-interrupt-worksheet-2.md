# Alcohol Numbing Interrupt: Action Builder

**Audience:** Healthcare Workers

**From guide:** Alcohol Numbing Interrupt

---

Goal: Interrupt the numbing pattern with one honest step.

Prompts:
- Tonight, when the urge hits, I'll do ______ instead for 10 minutes before deciding.
- The feeling I'm numbing is ______. One other way I could deal with it:
- One person I'd be willing to say "I think I'm drinking too much" to:
- My honest answer to: "Am I drinking to enjoy it or to stop feeling something?" 
- By this weekend, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
