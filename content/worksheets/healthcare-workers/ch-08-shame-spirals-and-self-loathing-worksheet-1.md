# Shame Spirals + Self-Loathing: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Healthcare Workers

**From guide:** Shame Spirals + Self-Loathing

---

Goal: Spot the pattern: when it shows up, what triggers it, and what you notice first.

Prompts:
- What are the three memories your brain loops most when shame takes over?
- What does shame tell you about yourself? Write it word for word.
- Who do you avoid when you're in the spiral?
- What's the difference between guilt (I did a bad thing) and shame (I am bad) in your head?
- When's the last time you told someone the truth about what you're carrying?
- What parts of clinical work or patient care are most connected to this pattern lately?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
