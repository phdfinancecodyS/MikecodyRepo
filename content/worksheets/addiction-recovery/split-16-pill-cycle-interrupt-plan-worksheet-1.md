# Pill-Cycle Interrupt Plan: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Addiction / Recovery

**From guide:** Pill-Cycle Interrupt Plan

---

Goal: Map the cycle: when you take more than prescribed and what you're chasing.

Prompts:
- What medication are you using more than prescribed? How much more?
- What triggers the extra dose: pain, anxiety, insomnia, emotional overwhelm, or habit?
- What does the extra dose give you? (Relief, calm, numbness, sleep, escape?)
- What does the cycle look like: overuse, run out early, scramble for refill, guilt, repeat?
- Who knows about this pattern? Who would you be most afraid to tell?
- What thoughts, feelings, or situations around this problem make you want to numb out, hide, or go back to old coping?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
