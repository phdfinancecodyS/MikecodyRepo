# Faith, God, Big Questions: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Addiction / Recovery

**From guide:** Faith, God, Big Questions

---

Goal: Map what changed between you and faith: the before, the break, and where you are now.

Prompts:
- What did your faith used to give you: comfort, community, identity, answers, structure?
- What broke it: a specific event, a slow erosion, or something someone said/did in God's name?
- What's the hardest part: losing the belief, losing the community, or losing who you were inside it?
- When you try to pray, attend, or practice now, what happens inside you?
- What question are you carrying that nobody in your faith tradition could answer?
- What thoughts, feelings, or situations around this problem make you want to numb out, hide, or go back to old coping?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
