# Shame Loop Repair: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Addiction / Recovery

**From guide:** Shame Loop Repair

---

Goal: Catch the shame loop: what it replays, what it says about you, and what it costs.

Prompts:
- What's the shame loop replaying right now? Write the exact thought.
- Is this about something you actually did, or something shame convinced you that you are?
- When did this shame start? Is it recent or has it been running for years?
- What do you do when the shame gets loud: hide, overfunction, self-punish, use, shut down?
- If you told the right person this story, what's the worst that could happen? What's the best?
- What thoughts, feelings, or situations around this problem make you want to numb out, hide, or go back to old coping?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
