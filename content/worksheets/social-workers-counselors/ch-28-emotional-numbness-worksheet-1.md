# Emotional Numbness: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Social Workers / Counselors

**From guide:** Emotional Numbness

---

Goal: Map the numbness: when it started, what it's protecting, and what leaks through.

Prompts:
- When did you stop feeling? Was it sudden or gradual?
- What's the last thing that made you feel anything: even briefly?
- What does your body feel like right now? Heavy, hollow, flat, or nothing at all?
- Who have you been pulling away from? What do they see when they look at you?
- Is there one feeling that still breaks through (anger, tears, irritation)? What triggers it?
- How does your role as a helper make this pattern easier to hide or explain away?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
