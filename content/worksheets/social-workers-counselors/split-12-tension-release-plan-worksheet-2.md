# Tension Release Plan: Action Builder

**Audience:** Social Workers / Counselors

**From guide:** Tension Release Plan

---

Goal: Build a daily tension-release practice: 60 seconds, three times a day.

Prompts:
- My three check-in times: ______, ______, ______.
- My release move for each (jaw drop, shoulder roll, fist clench-release, deep exhale): ______.
- One thing I'll address that's causing the tension (conversation, boundary, workload):
- One body-based practice I'll try this week (progressive muscle relaxation, stretching, bath, massage):
- By this weekend, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
