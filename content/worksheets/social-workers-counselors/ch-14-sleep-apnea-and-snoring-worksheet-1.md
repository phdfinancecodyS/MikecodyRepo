# Sleep Apnea and Snoring: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Social Workers / Counselors

**From guide:** Sleep Apnea and Snoring

---

Goal: Track the signs: what your body is telling you and what you've been ignoring.

Prompts:
- How tired are you during the day on a 1–10 scale? How long has it been that way?
- Does your partner say you snore, gasp, or stop breathing at night?
- What does "tired" look like for you: brain fog, irritability, falling asleep at your desk, something else?
- Have you had a sleep study? If not, what's stopped you?
- What other symptoms might be linked: headaches, weight changes, high blood pressure?
- How does your role as a helper make this pattern easier to hide or explain away?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
