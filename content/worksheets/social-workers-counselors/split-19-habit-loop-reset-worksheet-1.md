# Habit Loop Reset: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Social Workers / Counselors

**From guide:** Habit Loop Reset

---

Goal: Dissect the loop: identify the cue, the routine, and the reward your brain is chasing.

Prompts:
- What's the habit? Name it plainly, no softening.
- What's the cue: a time, place, emotion, person, or preceding action that starts it?
- What reward does the habit deliver? (relief, numbness, stimulation, comfort, control?)
- What have you tried to quit or change it? What happened?
- What does the habit cost you: time, health, relationships, money, self-respect?
- How does your role as a helper make this pattern easier to hide or explain away?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
