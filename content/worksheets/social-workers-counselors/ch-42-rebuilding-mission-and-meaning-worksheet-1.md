# Rebuilding Mission and Meaning: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Social Workers / Counselors

**From guide:** Rebuilding Mission and Meaning

---

Goal: Track where your sense of purpose went: and what's trying to replace it.

Prompts:
- What used to make you feel like you mattered? When did that stop working?
- What gets you out of bed now: obligation, habit, or nothing?
- What fills the hours that used to be filled with mission? Is it actually working?
- When's the last time you felt useful: genuinely useful, not just busy?
- What would you do if purpose showed up tomorrow? Would you trust it?
- How does your role as a helper make this pattern easier to hide or explain away?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
