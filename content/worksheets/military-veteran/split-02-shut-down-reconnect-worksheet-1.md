# Shut Down Reconnect: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Military / Veteran

**From guide:** Shut Down Reconnect

---

Goal: Map the shutdown pattern: what triggers it, how deep it goes, and what it costs.

Prompts:
- What usually triggers the shutdown: conflict, overwhelm, criticism, emotion, noise?
- When you shut down, what does it look like from the outside? What's happening inside?
- How long do you usually stay shut down: minutes, hours, days?
- What does the other person usually do when you go quiet? Does it help or make it worse?
- What would coming back look like if it didn't have to be a big thing?
- Where does training still help you, and where is it creating friction in civilian life or close relationships?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
