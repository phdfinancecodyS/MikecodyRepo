# Compulsion Interrupt Plan: Action Builder

**Audience:** Military / Veteran

**From guide:** Compulsion Interrupt Plan

---

Goal: Practice one delay and one redirect.

Prompts:
- The compulsion I'll target first: ______. My delay: ______ minutes before acting.
- My redirect when the urge hits (walk, breathe, call someone, change rooms): ______.
- My anxiety rating before waiting: ______/10. After 5 minutes of waiting: ______/10.
- One step toward professional support if this is ruling my day (ERP therapist, OCD specialist):
- By this weekend, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
