# Disclosure Repair: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Military / Veteran

**From guide:** Disclosure Repair

---

Goal: Map the disclosure: what you said (or need to say), to whom, and what happened (or might happen).

Prompts:
- What did you disclose (or need to disclose)? Write it in one sentence.
- Who did/will you tell? Why that person?
- If you already told them: what was their reaction? What did you need that you didn't get?
- If you haven't told them yet: what's the fear? What's the worst case? What's the likely case?
- What do you need from them: understanding, space, action, or just acknowledgment?
- Where does training still help you, and where is it creating friction in civilian life or close relationships?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
