# Tunnel Vision Reset: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Military / Veteran

**From guide:** Tunnel Vision Reset

---

Goal: Catch the narrowing: what your brain locks onto and what it's blocking out.

Prompts:
- What's the one thing your brain is stuck on right now? Write it down.
- What are three other things that are also true right now that your brain is ignoring?
- When did the tunnel start: and what was the trigger?
- What decision are you about to make from inside the tunnel? Would you make the same one tomorrow?
- Who are you not listening to right now because you're sure you already know?
- Where does training still help you, and where is it creating friction in civilian life or close relationships?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
