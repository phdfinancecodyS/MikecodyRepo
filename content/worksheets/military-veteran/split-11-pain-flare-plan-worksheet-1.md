# Pain Flare Plan: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Military / Veteran

**From guide:** Pain Flare Plan

---

Goal: Track the flare pattern: what triggers it, how bad it gets, and what you do when it hits.

Prompts:
- On a 1–10 scale, what's today's pain? What's your average? What's a "good day"?
- What triggered this flare: activity, weather, stress, sleep, or you don't know?
- When pain spikes, what happens to your mood, patience, and relationships?
- What have you tried that actually helps: even a little? What doesn't work but you keep doing?
- What does your family think you should do about the pain? What do you wish they understood?
- Where does training still help you, and where is it creating friction in civilian life or close relationships?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
