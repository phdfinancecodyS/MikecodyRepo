# Chronic Sleep Debt: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Military / Veteran

**From guide:** Chronic Sleep Debt

---

Goal: Spot the sleep pattern: when it breaks, what's driving it, and what you notice first.

Prompts:
- How long has the sleep debt been building? Weeks, months, or longer?
- What keeps you up: racing thoughts, screens, pain, noise, or habit?
- What does the exhaustion cost you during the day: mood, focus, patience, safety?
- What have you tried that helped even slightly? What made things worse?
- What time do you actually fall asleep vs. what time you get in bed?
- Where does training still help you, and where is it creating friction in civilian life or close relationships?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
