# Sleep Apnea and Snoring: Action Builder

**Audience:** BIPOC / Racial Trauma

**From guide:** Sleep Apnea and Snoring

---

Goal: Turn what you noticed into one medical step and one daily change.

Prompts:
- The one step I'll take this week toward a sleep study or CPAP follow-up:
- The sleeping position I'll try tonight (side sleeping, elevated head):
- What I'll track for the next week (wake-ups, energy at noon, headaches):
- The person I'll tell about this so they can hold me accountable:
- By next week, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
