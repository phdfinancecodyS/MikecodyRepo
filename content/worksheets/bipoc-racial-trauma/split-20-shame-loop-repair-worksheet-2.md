# Shame Loop Repair: Action Builder

**Audience:** BIPOC / Racial Trauma

**From guide:** Shame Loop Repair

---

Goal: Break the loop with one honest disclosure and one identity correction.

Prompts:
- The shame thought I'll say out loud (to myself, on paper, to someone safe): "______."
- The correction: "I did ______. That doesn't make me a ______." 
- One person I trust enough to tell a piece of this story to:
- One thing I'm proud of this week that shame wants me to erase:
- By next week, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
