# Shut Down Reconnect: Action Builder

**Audience:** BIPOC / Racial Trauma

**From guide:** Shut Down Reconnect

---

Goal: Build a re-entry plan so shutdown doesn't become permanent absence.

Prompts:
- My shutdown signal (to myself or someone safe): "I'm shutting down. Give me ______ and I'll come back."
- One physical action I'll use to break freeze (cold water, standing up, walking, stretching):
- One small re-entry action I'll do within ______ hours of shutting down (text, eye contact, showing up):
- The person I trust enough to tell about this pattern:
- By tomorrow, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
