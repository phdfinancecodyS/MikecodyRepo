# Eating Patterns Under Stress Reset Guide: Stress-Eating Pattern Map

**Audience:** Educators

**From guide:** Eating Patterns Under Stress Reset Guide

---

Goal: Understand exactly how stress is rewiring your eating so you can interrupt the pattern at the right point.

Prompts:
- When you're stressed, which direction does your eating go? (not eating, overeating, eating junk only, eating at random times, forgetting to eat entirely?)
- What does your eating look like on a typical high-stress day? Walk through breakfast/lunch/dinner. What did you actually eat yesterday?
- What feelings or situations trigger the pattern? (overwhelm, boredom, anger, numbness, shift work, not having time?)
- How is your body responding to the current eating pattern? (energy crashes, weight changes, stomach issues, mood swings, can't sleep?)
- What's your relationship with food like right now? One sentence. Be honest.
- Which moments in teaching or school life are most linked to this issue right now?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
