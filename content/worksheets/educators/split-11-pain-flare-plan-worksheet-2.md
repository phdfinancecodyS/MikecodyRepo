# Pain Flare Plan: Action Builder

**Audience:** Educators

**From guide:** Pain Flare Plan

---

Goal: Build a flare-day plan so you don't have to think during the worst of it.

Prompts:
- My flare-day essentials (ice/heat, position, medication, timing):
- One thing I'll cancel or reschedule when pain hits a 7+:
- The sentence I'll say to people around me: "I'm in a flare. I need ______."
- One comfort I'll allow myself without guilt on pain days:
- By this weekend, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
