# Parenting on Empty: Action Builder

**Audience:** Chronic Illness / Chronic Pain

**From guide:** Parenting on Empty

---

Goal: Protect one parenting anchor and get help with the rest.

Prompts:
- The one non-negotiable parenting moment I'll protect this week:
- The task I'll ask for help with (and from whom):
- The guilt thought I'll challenge when it shows up: "I should be able to ______"
- One thing I'll let go of this week without guilt:
- By tomorrow night, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
