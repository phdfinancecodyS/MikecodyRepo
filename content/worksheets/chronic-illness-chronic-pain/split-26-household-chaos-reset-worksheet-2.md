# Household Chaos Reset: Action Builder

**Audience:** Chronic Illness / Chronic Pain

**From guide:** Household Chaos Reset

---

Goal: Clear one space. Feel the difference.

Prompts:
- The one surface I'll clear right now: ______.
- Tomorrow's 15-minute room: ______. Timer starts at ______.
- One system I'll put in place (evening reset, dish rule, laundry schedule): ______.
- Help I'll ask for: "Can you handle ______ this week while I handle ______?"
- By this weekend, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
