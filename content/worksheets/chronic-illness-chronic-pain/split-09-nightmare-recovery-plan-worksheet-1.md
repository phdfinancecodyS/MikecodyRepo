# Nightmare Recovery Plan: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** Chronic Illness / Chronic Pain

**From guide:** Nightmare Recovery Plan

---

Goal: Track the nightmare pattern: what's happening at night and what it's doing to your days.

Prompts:
- Describe the most frequent nightmare. What happens? How does it end?
- Does the nightmare replay something real, or is it a new scenario built from old feelings?
- What does your body do when you wake up? (racing heart, sweating, frozen, crying, can't move)
- How long does it take you to come back to the present? What helps?
- What are you doing to avoid sleep because of the nightmares?
- What symptoms, limits, or energy patterns are most connected to this issue?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
