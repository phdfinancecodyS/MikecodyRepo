# Overwhelm Stabilization Plan: Action Builder

**Audience:** High Stress Jobs

**From guide:** Overwhelm Stabilization Plan

---

Goal: Do one thing. Then decide the next one.

Prompts:
- The one thing I'll do first right now: ______. It'll take ______ minutes.
- The two things I'll do tomorrow (one before noon, one after): ______ and ______.
- One thing I'll cancel, delegate, or let wait: ______.
- One person I'll tell "I'm overwhelmed" to: ______.
- By tomorrow night, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
