# Compulsion Interrupt Plan: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** High Stress Jobs

**From guide:** Compulsion Interrupt Plan

---

Goal: Map the compulsion cycle: trigger, urge, behavior, relief, repeat.

Prompts:
- What's the compulsion: checking, counting, cleaning, repeating, arranging, something else?
- What triggers it: a specific thought, situation, feeling, or "something just feels wrong"?
- What happens if you don't do it? What does your brain tell you will happen?
- How many times do you do it before the anxiety drops? Is it getting more or less?
- What has the compulsion cost you (time, relationships, energy, sanity)?
- What parts of your job predictably make this problem spike?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
