# Crisis Conversation Action: Action Builder

**Audience:** High Stress Jobs

**From guide:** Crisis Conversation Action

---

Goal: Plan the conversation: when, where, and what you'll say.

Prompts:
- The person I'll talk to: ______. When: ______. Where: ______.
- My opening sentence: "I care about you and I need to ask you something directly."
- If they say yes, I'll say: "Thank you for telling me. Let's get you connected to support right now."
- The crisis resource I'll have ready: 988 (call/text) or 741741 (text HOME).
- After the conversation, who I'll debrief with so I'm not carrying this alone:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
