# Stress Shuts Down Sex: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** General Mental Health

**From guide:** Stress Shuts Down Sex

---

Goal: Connect the stress to the shutdown: what's between you and your body's ability to connect.

Prompts:
- What's your biggest stressor right now? How does it show up in your body?
- When stress is high, does your body shut down desire entirely or just make it harder to get there?
- What does your partner think the problem is? What's really happening?
- When's the last time stress was low enough that desire showed up naturally? What was different?
- What happens when you try to push through: does it work or make it worse?
- What part of this problem is most active in your life right now?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us.
