# Household Chaos Reset: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** General Mental Health

**From guide:** Household Chaos Reset

---

Goal: Understand the connection between the chaos outside and what's happening inside.

Prompts:
- How bad is it right now: honestly? What areas are worst?
- When did the house start slipping? What else was happening in your life at that time?
- Is the mess about laziness (probably not) or depletion (probably yes)?
- What does the mess make you feel: shame, overwhelm, numbness, anger, nothing?
- What would one clean room do for your mental state right now?
- What part of this problem is most active in your life right now?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
