# Crisis Conversation Action: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** General Mental Health

**From guide:** Crisis Conversation Action

---

Goal: Prepare for the conversation: name what you're seeing and what's stopping you from asking.

Prompts:
- Who are you worried about? What specific behavior or change made you notice?
- What are you most afraid will happen if you ask directly about suicide?
- What are you afraid will happen if you don't ask?
- Have you had a crisis conversation before? What happened?
- What's stopping you from asking today: fear of making it worse, not knowing the words, or something else?
- What part of this problem is most active in your life right now?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
