# Home Feels Like a Minefield: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** General Mental Health

**From guide:** Home Feels Like a Minefield

---

Goal: Map the minefield: what triggers you at home and what you're actually scanning for.

Prompts:
- Which room or situation at home creates the most tension?
- What are you scanning for: someone's mood, tone of voice, footsteps, silence?
- How does your body feel when you walk through the door? Tight, braced, exhausted?
- Who in the home do you monitor most? What are you afraid will happen?
- When's the last time you fully relaxed at home without being on alert?
- What part of this problem is most active in your life right now?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
