# Lone Wolf to Healthy Team Member: Action Builder

**Audience:** General Mental Health

**From guide:** Lone Wolf to Healthy Team Member

---

Goal: Let one person in: one small moment of team, not total surrender.

Prompts:
- One task I'll delegate or share this week instead of doing alone:
- One honest sentence I'll say: "I could use help with ______." (Start small.)
- What I'll notice in my body when I let someone help (relief, anxiety, gratitude, panic):
- One person who's earned enough trust for me to practice this with:
- By next week, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
