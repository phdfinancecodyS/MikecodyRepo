# Secondary Trauma for Partners: Pattern Finder

**Audience:** General Mental Health

**From guide:** Secondary Trauma for Partners

---

Goal: Track what you've absorbed: separate your partner's pain from yours.

Prompts:
- What symptoms of theirs have become yours? (Anxiety, hypervigilance, sleep problems, irritability?)
- When did you stop taking care of yourself? What was happening with them at that time?
- What do you pretend is fine that isn't?
- What would you say to a friend in your position? Why is it harder to say it to yourself?
- When's the last time you did something just because it made you happy?
- What part of this problem is most active in your life right now?

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
