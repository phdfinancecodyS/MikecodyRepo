# Blow Up Recovery: Action Builder

**Audience:** Neurodivergent

**From guide:** Blow Up Recovery

---

Goal: Build a repair plan: not an excuse, a real repair.

Prompts:
- One sentence I'll say to the person I blew up at: "I lost control, and I'm sorry for ______."
- My body's earliest warning sign that I'm about to blow (heart rate, jaw, voice, heat):
- One interrupt I'll use next time (leave room, cold water, count to 10, say "I need a minute"):
- What I'll do in the 90 seconds after I interrupt (walk, breathe, text someone, splash face):
- By tomorrow, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
