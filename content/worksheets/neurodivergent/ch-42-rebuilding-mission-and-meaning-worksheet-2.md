# Rebuilding Mission and Meaning: Action Builder

**Audience:** Neurodivergent

**From guide:** Rebuilding Mission and Meaning

---

Goal: Take one small purpose-driven action this week: not a career plan, just one moment of meaning.

Prompts:
- One thing I'll do this week where someone benefits from my effort (volunteer, mentor, build, teach, help):
- One skill or strength I have that's going unused right now:
- The sentence I'll practice: "I don't need a title to ______."
- One person who needs what I know: I'll reach out to them:
- By next week, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
