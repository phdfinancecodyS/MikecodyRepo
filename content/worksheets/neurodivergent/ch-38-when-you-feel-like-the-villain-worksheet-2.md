# When You Feel Like the Villain: Action Builder

**Audience:** Neurodivergent

**From guide:** When You Feel Like the Villain

---

Goal: Challenge the villain narrative with one honest action.

Prompts:
- One thing I did wrong that I'll own without adding "and that makes me a terrible person":
- One thing I did right recently that the villain narrative wants me to erase:
- The sentence I'll practice: "I did ______. That doesn't mean I am ______." 
- One person I'll let see me without the villain mask this week:
- By next week, I'll know this worked if:

---

**A quick note:** This worksheet is educational: it's not therapy, it's not a diagnosis, and using it doesn't create a clinical relationship between us. It's built to help you take the next step, not replace professional support. If you're in crisis, contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), or call 911.
