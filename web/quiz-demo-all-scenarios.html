Quiz Questions - "For Me" Track

2 questions. Q1 = overall state. Q2 = safety question (suicidal ideation).
Override rules: Q2 = 3 → force Critical. Q2 = 2 → minimum High Risk.
Question 1 of 2

How are you really doing right now?

I'm doing okay - some stress, but I'm managing
I'm struggling more than usual - low energy, poor sleep, feeling disconnected
I'm in real pain - overwhelmed, hopeless, or barely getting through the day
I feel like I'm in crisis - I can't function and I need help now
Question 2 of 2

Have you had thoughts about hurting yourself or that you'd be better off dead?

No, not at all
I've had a fleeting thought, but it passed
I've had these thoughts more than once and they're hard to shake [→ force High Risk]
I think about it constantly or I have a plan [→ force Critical]

Quiz Questions - "For Someone" Track

2 questions. Q1 = observed changes. Q2 = direct concern about suicide/self-harm.
Same override rules: Q2 = 3 → force Critical. Q2 = 2 → minimum High Risk.
Question 1 of 2

What changes have you noticed in the person you're worried about?

They seem a little off, but mostly themselves
They've been more withdrawn, moody, or not acting like themselves
They've changed a lot - isolating, sleeping differently, losing interest in everything
They've given things away, said goodbye, talked about being a burden, or mentioned not wanting to be here
Question 2 of 2

Has this person talked about suicide, self-harm, or not wanting to be alive?

No, nothing like that
They've made vague comments that made me uneasy
They've said things that really worried me, more than once [→ force High Risk]
They've told me they have a plan, or they've attempted before [→ force Critical]

Scoring Reference

2 questions × 0–3 each = 0–6 total.

0–1 = Low Risk    2–3 = Moderate Risk    4–5 = High Risk    6 = Critical

Override rules (Q2 - the safety question):
• Q2 answer = 3 → force Critical regardless of total score
• Q2 answer = 2 → minimum High Risk regardless of total score

Safety order on result pages:
• Low / Moderate: capture → products → crisis footer
• High: crisis CTAs first → capture → secondary products
• Critical: crisis CTAs first → if-you-can't section → capture (after crisis only) → NO products
